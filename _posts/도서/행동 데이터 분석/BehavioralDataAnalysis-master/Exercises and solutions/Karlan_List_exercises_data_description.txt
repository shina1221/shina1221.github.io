The file Karlan_List.csv contains the following variables:
- gave: binary target variable, 1 if the donor gave after receiving the letter, 0 otherwise
- amount: numeric target variable, amount given by the donor after receiving the letter
- group: categorical variable for experimental group, “control”, treat1”, “treat2”, or “treat3”
- freq: numeric variable, frequency of past donations 
- gender: categorical variable, donor gender, “male”, “female” or “couple”
- state_pol: categorical variable, political leaning of the donor’s state (“red” or “blue”)
- county_pol: categorical variable, political leaning of the donor’s county (“red” or “blue”)
- dormant: binary variable, 1 if the donor previously gave this year, 0 otherwise

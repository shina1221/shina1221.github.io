The file Bertrand_Mullainathan.csv contains the following variables:
- call: binary target variable, 0 when the hiring firm did not call back the candidate, 1 when the hiring firm called back the candidate
- education: numeric variable, number of years of education
- yearsexp: numeric variable, number of years of experience
- race: categorical variable, 'b' for black-sounding names, 'w' for white-sounding names
- sex: categorical variable, 'f' for female, 'm' for male
- college: binary variable, 0 when the applicant didn't go to college, 1 when the applicant went to college
- eoe: binary variable, 0 when the hiring firm is not an Equal Opportunity Employer, 1 when the hiring firm is an Equal Opportunity Employer
